About DPS Toba Tek Singh

Divisional Public School & College Toba Tek Singh is one of the leading educational institutions dedicated to academic excellence, character building and leadership development. The school provides a modern learning environment where students are encouraged to achieve their highest potential in academics, sports and co-curricular activities.


Our mission is to develop confident, responsible and creative individuals who contribute positively to society and become future leaders.
